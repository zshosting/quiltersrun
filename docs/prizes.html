Drawing
The prize drawing for the Southern California Quilter Run (SCQR) will take place in accordance with the terms outlined on this web page. Alternate winners will be chosen in the event a selected entry is not valid.

Eligibility / How to Enter
Participants must be 18-years of age or older. In addition, participants must visit every store and receive a stamp on their passport. All participants that meet the eligibility requirements are auto entered in the prize drawings.

How to Win
Southern California Quilt Run Association (SCQRA) will randomly select winners from the eligible entries received. Using the contact information provided on the Passport, SCQRA will notify winners by phone. Participants must provide their true, full legal name and phone number when turning in their passport.

Odds
The odds of wining any prize vary based on the total number of entries that are received. Players may not win more than one prize.
